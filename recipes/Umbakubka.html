Libyan Umbakubka

Odin Recipes

Mbakbaka is about to become your spicy Arabic pasta obsession — this dish is a full-on flavor explosion! It’s not only packed with all the right spices but it also boasts the perfect texture. And the best part? It’s a breeze to whip up! Get ready to fall head over heels for this easy, spicy one-pot pasta!

Ingredients

  • 1 onion
  • 4 cloves of garlic
  • 4 chillies
  • 1 kg of lamb meat
  • 4 tomatoes blended
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • salt and pepper
  • 1 tablespoon turmeric
  • 1 tablspoon dried basil
  • 5 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 to 3 cups pasta

Steps

  1. start by frying the onion in the olive oil
  2. then add the meat with all the spices and tomato paste.
  3. once aromatic add the 4 blended tomatoes with a cup of water and let it simmer on medium heat till the meat is tender. For about 40 minutes.
  4. add the pasta with 2 cups of water and let the pasta cook if you find it too thick you can adjust it with water till you reach the consistency you prefer.
